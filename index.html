Research Initiative

Understanding how law firms are interpreted, discovered and recommended in AI environments.

AI is changing how legal expertise is surfaced. Legal AI Discovery explores how law firms become discoverable, understandable and recommendable in emerging AI systems.

The Core Question

How do law firms become discoverable in AI?

As AI systems become a growing layer between expertise and clients, discoverability may become increasingly important. This project studies the mechanisms behind AI-mediated legal discovery.
