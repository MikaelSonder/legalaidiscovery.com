KINGDOM OF SWEDEN
OBJECT DEFINITION
DEFINITIONThe statutory accounting function responsible for the ongoing, complete, traceable and evidence-based recording of business transactions, the maintenance of verifications and accounting records, and the preservation of räkenskapsinformation in accordance with Swedish bookkeeping law, annual accounts law and generally accepted accounting practice.

SWEDISH ACCOUNTING MODEL
SWEDISH MODELSwedish bookkeeping is a legally structured and principle-guided system. BFN develops good accounting practice through guidance on bookkeeping, verifications, archiving of räkenskapsinformation, systemdokumentation and behandlingshistorik, while the bookkeeping records must remain complete, timely and traceable.
INTERNATIONAL POSITIONThe system produces Swedish-law-compliant accounting information that can serve as a reliable basis for taxation, ownership reporting, financing and cross-border business analysis.
