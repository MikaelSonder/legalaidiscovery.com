KINGDOM OF SWEDEN
OBJECT DEFINITION
DEFINITIONThe legally governed professional function responsible for the complete, timely and traceable recording, classification, verification, preservation and retrieval of accounting information for entities subject to Swedish bookkeeping requirements, in accordance with Bokföringslagen, Årsredovisningslagen and generally accepted accounting practice.
